This is my work for week 40 of the #MakeoverMonday project.

This week's dataset focuses on avocado prices in the USA, particularly on their increase due to tropical storms and other natural events. The original article reports a lot of information, and is really interesting to read. Due to a shortage of time, I only did a quick and dirty plot of the trend in avocado prices in different American areas over time.

The original data are provided by Hass Avocado Board, and data used for this dashboard can be downloaded from data.world.

The original dashboard I produced is available on my Tableau Public profile.
