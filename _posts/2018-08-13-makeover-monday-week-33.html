This is my work for week 33 of the #MakeoverMonday project.

This week's dataset described all cities visited by the chef Anthony Bourdain during his many TV shows, and came from @christinezhang. I focused on the places he travelled to for his shows, highlighting the number of episodes shot in each city and country.

The original visualization can be found in one of @christinezhang's tweets, and data can be downloaded from data.world.

The original dashboard I produced is available on my Tableau Public profile.
