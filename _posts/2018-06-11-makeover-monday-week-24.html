This is my work for week 24 of the #MakeoverMonday project.

This week's dataset was focused on the Tourism Density Index, a numeric index used to quantify the number of residents vs tourists for each country. Some countries are subject to overtourism, which is when visitors outweigh locals, while others experience undertourism, meaning that the number of tourists is way lower than that of residents.

More information on can be found in the original article, and data can be downloaded from data.world.

The original dashboard I produced is available on my Tableau Public profile.
